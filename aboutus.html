About Us

We are a dog grooming salon in Ottawa, and our goal is to provide the best experience for our customers. We don't use cages, and we will adapt, to the best of our ability, to all the needs of different dogs.

In order to prioritize your dog's wellbeing and safety, we have made the following policies:

  • The groomer has the right to conclude the grooming process if the dog becomes extremely anxious or otherwise shows signs of aggression.
  • We will notify you 20 minutes before the conclusion of the grooming process. Please refrain from waiting at the shop. In addition, please pick up your dog within two hours of the notification, or additional charges will be incurred.
